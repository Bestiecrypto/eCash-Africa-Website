About eCash

eCash is an optimized version of Bitcoin, focusing on innovation, independence, and security. It functions as digital money, free from corporate influence, and offers low transaction fees. eCash aims to provide a more accessible and user-friendly cryptocurrency experience for everyone. 😊

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote financial inclusion, innovation, and adoption of eCash in Africa. We focus on spreading awareness,supporting development, and encouraging the use of eCash's tech for various applications
